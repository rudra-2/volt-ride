How do I rent an EV?

You can rent an EV through our mobile app by selecting your preferred vehicle, verifying your details, and making a payment.

What are the rental charges?

We offer a pay-per-km pricing model. You can check the rates in the pricing section of our website or app.

How do I unlock the EV?

You can unlock your EV using the unique QR code or RFID access provided in the app.

What if my EV runs out of charge?

Our app provides real-time battery status and nearby charging stations for your convenience.

Can I pre-book an EV?

Yes, you can pre-book an EV for a future ride using our app.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our support team through the app or visit the 'Contact Us' section on our website.

Need Assistance During Your Ride?

Facing an issue with your EV? Our expert support team is available 24/7 to help you get back on the road quickly and safely.
